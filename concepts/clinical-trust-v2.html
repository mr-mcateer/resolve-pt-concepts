Conditions We Treat

Post-Operative Rehabilitation

Recovery from hip, knee, shoulder, and spinal surgery with protocol-driven, hands-on care. Our therapists have observed surgical procedures firsthand to better understand your recovery.

Learn More →

Orthopedic Injuries & Manual Therapy

Expert assessment and hands-on treatment for sprains, strains, fractures, and joint dysfunction. We find the root cause — not just manage symptoms.

Learn More →

Vestibular Rehab & Vertigo

Specialized treatment for BPPV, dizziness, and balance disorders. Targeted repositioning maneuvers and vestibular exercises to restore your equilibrium.

Learn More →

Running Rehabilitation & Gait Analysis

Video gait analysis and DorsaVi wearable biomechanics to identify movement patterns causing injury. Evidence-based return-to-run programming.

Learn More →

Chronic Pain Management

Comprehensive evaluation and treatment for persistent pain conditions. Combining manual therapy, therapeutic exercise, and patient education.

Learn More →

Shockwave Therapy

Advanced extracorporeal shockwave technology for tendinopathy, plantar fasciitis, and soft tissue healing.

Learn More →

Our Approach to Your Recovery

1

Assess

Comprehensive evaluation to identify the true source of your pain — not just the symptoms. We listen first, then act.

2

Treat

Hands-on manual therapy combined with individualized exercise programs. Your therapist stays with you start to finish.

3

Educate

Understanding your body's mechanics so you can prevent re-injury and stay active long-term. We teach you the “why.”
