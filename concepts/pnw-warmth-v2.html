Patient Information

We want your first visit to be as smooth as possible. Here are a few things to know before you arrive.

Insurance & Billing

We accept most major insurances including Medicare and Medicaid/OHP. We verify your benefits before your first appointment so there are no surprises.

What to Wear

Comfortable, loose-fitting athletic clothing that allows access to the area being treated. Running shoes if we are performing gait analysis.

Your First Visit

Your initial evaluation is a full 60 minutes one-on-one with your therapist. We discuss your history, assess your movement, and begin treatment right away.
