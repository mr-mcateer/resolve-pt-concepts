Conditions We Treat

Post-Operative Rehabilitation

Comprehensive protocol adherence for hip, knee, shoulder, and spine surgeries. We work directly with your referring surgeon to ensure you meet key milestones safely and efficiently, reducing the risk of complications.

Learn More →

Orthopedic Injuries & Manual Therapy

From acute joint sprains to chronic tendinopathies, our Doctors of Physical Therapy utilize evidence-based manual manipulation to restore joint mechanics, reduce inflammation, and accelerate tissue healing.

Learn More →

Vestibular Rehab & Vertigo

Specialized neurological testing and repositioning maneuvers (such as the Epley maneuver) to target and resolve BPPV, dizziness, and related balance disorders safely.

Meet Your Providers

Owner & Founder

Jenny McAteer, DPT

Dr. McAteer founded Resolve Physical Therapy with a commitment to maintaining the highest standard of one-on-one care. Her clinical expertise centers around complex post-surgical protocols and biomechanical corrections using advanced DorsaVi wearable sensors.

Endurance Specialist

Nate Palmer, DPT

Dr. Palmer provides evidence-based rehabilitation for serious endurance athletes. Having completed the Pacific Crest Trail, he understands the physiological demands of extreme distance and focuses on returning patients to high-impact activities safely.
