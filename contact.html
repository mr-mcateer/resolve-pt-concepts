Insurance & Payment

We accept most major insurance plans including Medicare and Medicaid/OHP. Call to verify your benefits before your first visit.

If you are unsure whether your plan is accepted, our front office team is happy to help verify your coverage. We want the financial side of physical therapy to be as straightforward as possible so you can focus on getting better.
