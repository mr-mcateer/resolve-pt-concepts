Our Physical Therapy Services

Comprehensive, evidence-based physical therapy in Bend, Oregon. Every session is one-on-one with a Doctor of Physical Therapy who listens, evaluates, and treats—because your recovery deserves undivided attention.

  1. Home

At Resolve Physical Therapy, we believe that healing is personal. Founded in 2018 by Jenny McAteer, DPT, our clinic in Bend, Oregon provides individualized, one-on-one physical therapy for patients of every age and activity level. Whether you are recovering from surgery, managing chronic pain, training for your next race, or navigating a complex condition like Ehlers-Danlos Syndrome, our team of Doctors of Physical Therapy will build a treatment plan tailored to your body, your goals, and your life.

We accept most major insurance plans, including Medicare and Medicaid/OHP, and we never require a physician referral to get started. Oregon is a direct-access state, which means you can schedule your evaluation today and begin treatment right away.

Services We Offer

Specialty

Running & Sports Performance

DorsaVi biomechanical sensor analysis, video gait evaluation, and personalized return-to-sport programs for runners and athletes of every level.

Post-Surgical Rehabilitation

Structured recovery programs following joint replacement, ACL reconstruction, rotator cuff repair, spinal surgery, and other orthopedic procedures.

Vestibular & Balance Therapy

Specialized assessment and treatment for vertigo, dizziness, BPPV, concussion-related balance issues, and fall prevention in older adults.

Chronic Pain & Manual Therapy

Hands-on manual therapy, pain neuroscience education, therapeutic exercise, and graded exposure techniques for persistent pain conditions.
New Service

Ehlers-Danlos Syndrome

Gentle joint stabilization, proprioception training, and hypermobility management for patients with EDS and Hypermobility Spectrum Disorder.
First Session Free

Focused Shockwave Therapy

Acoustic wave technology for plantar fasciitis, Achilles tendinopathy, calcific tendinitis, and other chronic tendon conditions that resist conventional treatment.

Neuro Rehabilitation

Evidence-based neurological rehabilitation for stroke recovery, Parkinson's disease, multiple sclerosis, and traumatic brain injury with functional movement training.
