Patient Information

We want your first visit to be as smooth as possible. Here are a few things to know before you arrive.

Insurance & Billing

We accept most major insurances including Medicare and Medicaid/OHP. We will verify your benefits prior to your first appointment so there are no surprises.

What to Wear

Please wear comfortable, loose-fitting athletic clothing that allows access to the area we are treating. Running shoes are recommended if we are performing gait analysis.

Your First Visit

Your initial evaluation will be a full 60 minutes one-on-one with your therapist. We will discuss your history, assess your movement, and begin treatment immediately.
