About Kasey

Kasey Lohman is a Central Oregon native who grew up in Redmond, just minutes north of Bend. After spending years away pursuing his education, Kasey returned to the region he loves to build his career in physical therapy at Resolve Physical Therapy, where he has been a valued member of the team since 2021. His deep roots in the area give him a genuine understanding of the active, outdoor-driven lifestyle that defines Central Oregon, and that connection resonates with every patient he treats.

Kasey earned his Bachelor of Science in Exercise and Sport Science from Oregon State University, where he built a strong foundation in human movement, exercise physiology, and the science of athletic performance. He then pursued his Doctorate of Physical Therapy from the University of St. Augustine for Health Sciences in Austin, Texas, one of the nation's respected programs for hands-on clinical training. This combination of undergraduate exercise science and graduate-level physical therapy education gives Kasey a particularly well-rounded perspective when working with patients, especially athletes and active individuals who need a therapist who speaks the language of sport and movement.

Working with Athletes

Kasey has developed a strong focus on treating youth and collegiate athletes, a population that requires a nuanced and specialized approach. Young athletes are still growing, and their musculoskeletal systems respond differently to injury, training loads, and rehabilitation compared to adult patients. Kasey understands these developmental considerations and tailors his treatment plans accordingly, ensuring that young athletes recover safely without compromising their long-term joint health or athletic potential. Whether it is a high school soccer player dealing with an ACL sprain, a collegiate cross-country runner battling shin splints, or a young gymnast working through a chronic shoulder issue, Kasey meets each athlete where they are and builds a plan that gets them back to competition at their best.

His approach with athletes extends beyond just addressing the immediate injury. Kasey takes the time to educate his patients and their families about why the injury occurred in the first place, what movement patterns or training habits may have contributed, and what proactive steps can prevent recurrence. This educational component is central to how Kasey practices, and it empowers athletes to take ownership of their bodies and their recovery. He believes that a well-informed patient is a patient who heals faster and stays healthier over the long term.

Chronic Pain Management

Beyond his work with athletes, Kasey brings significant skill to the treatment of chronic pain. Chronic pain conditions present unique challenges because the relationship between tissue damage and pain experience is often complex and multifactorial. Kasey takes a patient-centered approach to chronic pain that acknowledges the physical, neurological, and psychological components of persistent pain. He uses a combination of manual therapy techniques, graded exposure to movement, and targeted strengthening exercises to help patients gradually reclaim function and reduce their pain levels over time.

His hands-on manual therapy skills are a particular strength. Kasey is adept at joint mobilizations, soft tissue techniques, and neuromuscular re-education, all of which play a critical role in reducing pain, restoring mobility, and improving motor control. Patients consistently appreciate his ability to identify the specific structures contributing to their symptoms and his skill in applying the right manual intervention to create immediate change that is then reinforced with targeted exercise.

The Resolve Approach

At Resolve Physical Therapy, Kasey fully embraces the clinic's one-on-one treatment model. He values the ability to spend the entire session with his patients, building the kind of therapeutic relationship that is simply not possible in high-volume clinic settings. This dedicated time allows Kasey to perform thorough reassessments at every visit, adjust treatment plans in real time based on how the patient is responding, and provide the hands-on care and detailed exercise instruction that produce lasting results. His patients describe him as attentive, thorough, and genuinely invested in their progress.

Outside the Clinic

He is an avid hunter and fisherman who takes full advantage of the incredible access to public lands, rivers, and high-desert terrain that surround Bend and Redmond. Whether he is spending a crisp fall morning in the field or casting on the Deschutes or Crooked River, Kasey values his time in the outdoors as a way to recharge and stay connected to the landscape he grew up exploring.

Beyond the outdoors, Kasey is a self-described gearhead who enjoys working on vehicles in his spare time, a hobby that demands the same patience and problem-solving skills he brings to his clinical practice. He also has a genuine passion for cooking, often experimenting with new recipes and techniques that keep things interesting in the kitchen. Kasey lives with his partner Ashley and their two dogs, Jack and Moose, who make sure he stays active and entertained at home.
