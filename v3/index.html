Why Resolve Physical Therapy?

True 1:1 Care

Every session, start to finish, with your dedicated DPT. No handoffs to aides or assistants — ever.

Evidence-Based Treatment

DorsaVi wearable sensors, video gait analysis, and biomechanical testing to find the root cause — not just treat symptoms.

Built for Active People

Our team lives here because they love to ski, bike, run, and paddle. We understand what getting back to the trail really means.

Specialized Services

Running Rehabilitation

Video gait analysis, DorsaVi wearable sensors, and AxIT force plates for data-driven rehab. The most advanced biomechanical assessment in Central Oregon.

Post-Surgical Recovery

Coordinated protocols with your surgeon. ACL reconstruction, rotator cuff repair, joint replacement — all with hands-on 1:1 care.

Vestibular & Vertigo Therapy

BPPV, dizziness, and balance disorders. Specialized assessment and treatment to restore your equilibrium and confidence.

Chronic Pain Management

Root-cause approach combining manual therapy, targeted exercise, and patient education. Lasting solutions, not temporary relief.

Joint Replacement Rehab

Pre-hab and post-op protocols for hip, knee, and shoulder replacements. Structured progression for optimal outcomes.

Sports Injury Recovery

Return-to-sport protocols for athletes of all levels. Youth to masters — with evidence-based timelines and objective benchmarks.

Focused Shockwave Therapy

Cutting-edge focused shockwave technology for chronic tendon and soft tissue conditions. Plantar fasciitis, Achilles tendinopathy, tennis elbow. First session free.

Ehlers-Danlos Syndrome

Specialized treatment for EDS and hypermobility spectrum disorders with Sarah Consiglio, DPT. Joint stabilization, pain management, and functional strengthening.
Flagship Specialty

Meet Your Therapists

Jenny McAteer, DPT

Founder / Owner

Jenny has been a Physical Therapist since 2010, earning her Doctorate from Idaho State University. She founded Resolve PT in 2018 with one mission: true 1:1 care for every patient. Jenny specializes in whiplash recovery, post-operative rehabilitation, running rehab with DorsaVi wearable sensors, and vestibular therapy. Her approach combines hands-on manual therapy with patient education on body mechanics and home exercises.

Running Rehab Vestibular Post-Op DorsaVi Sensors

When she's not in the clinic, Jenny is hiking, camping, paddle boarding, and mountain biking with her husband Nick and daughters Natalie and Evelyn.

Kasey Lohman, DPT

Physical Therapist

A Central Oregon native from Redmond, Kasey earned his B.S. in Exercise and Sport Science from Oregon State University before completing his Doctorate at the University of St. Augustine for Health Sciences. Since joining Resolve in 2021, Kasey has built a reputation for connecting with athletes of all ages. He specializes in treating high school and collegiate athletes, cheerleaders, gymnasts, dancers, and individuals with chronic pain through patient education, manual therapy, and motor control training.

Youth Athletes Manual Therapy Chronic Pain Motor Control

Kasey lives with his partner Ashley and their dogs Jack and Moose. Outside the clinic, he enjoys hunting, fishing, working on vehicles, and cooking.

Sarah Consiglio, DPT

Physical Therapist

Sarah earned her Doctorate from OSU-Cascades and holds a B.S. in Psychology from Southern Oregon University. She brings a unique perspective to rehabilitation, specializing in post-operative recovery, hypermobility management, chronic pain, and pediatric orthopedics — including children with cerebral palsy, hip dysplasia, limb differences, and toe walking. Sarah is passionate about adaptive sports and empowering every patient to move with confidence.

Pediatric Ortho Post-Op Hypermobility Adaptive Sports

Sarah is married with one dog. She loves hiking, camping, paddle boarding, crafting, cooking, and baking.

Ethan Bruno, DPT

Physical Therapist

Before becoming a physical therapist, Ethan was a kindergarten teacher — and that background in education shows in every session. Originally from Washington, D.C. (with stops in Portland and Flagstaff), Ethan takes a hands-on, personalized approach focused on education, movement mechanics, and functional recovery. He specializes in musculoskeletal conditions and is dedicated to helping patients achieve lasting results, not just temporary relief.

Musculoskeletal Movement Education Functional Recovery

Ethan loves skiing, biking, soccer, ultimate frisbee, and ceramics.
