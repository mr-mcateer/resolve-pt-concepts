Why Resolve Physical Therapy?

At Resolve Physical Therapy, we built a clinic around a simple idea: every patient deserves the full, undivided attention of their therapist for the entire visit. No assistants, no aides, no double-booking. Just you and your patient, every session.

Founded in 2018 by Jenny McAteer, DPT, Resolve PT has grown into a team of dedicated therapists who share a commitment to evidence-based, patient-centered care. We treat a wide range of conditions including running injuries, post-surgical rehab, vestibular disorders, chronic pain, and Ehlers-Danlos Syndrome. Our therapists have access to advanced technology like DorsaVi biomechanical sensors and focused shockwave therapy.

Our Values

  • True 1:1 Care — No shared appointments, no hand-offs
  • Find the Source — Treat root causes, not just symptoms
  • Evidence-Based Practice — Continuing education is encouraged and supported
  • Community — We live and play in Bend, and our patients are our neighbors
  • Collaborative Team — We learn from each other and support growth

PRN/On-Call Physical Therapist

Now Hiring

We are looking for a PRN/On-Call Physical Therapist to join our growing team. Resolve PT provides true one-on-one care with no assistants or aides. You will treat patients individually in a supportive, collaborative environment where your clinical judgment is respected and your professional growth is encouraged.

Requirements

  • Doctor of Physical Therapy (DPT) degree from an accredited program
  • Oregon Physical Therapy license (or eligible for licensure)
  • Passion for evidence-based, patient-centered care
  • Strong communication skills and a collaborative mindset
  • Interest in manual therapy, therapeutic exercise, and patient education

What We Offer

  • Work-life balance in Bend, Oregon — access to world-class skiing, mountain biking, hiking, and more
  • Collaborative, supportive team environment
  • Continuing education support and encouragement
  • Access to advanced tools including DorsaVi sensors and focused shockwave therapy
  • True one-on-one patient care model — no double-booking, no aides
  • Flexible PRN scheduling
