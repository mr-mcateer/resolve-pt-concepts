Our Story

In 2018, Jenny McAteer, DPT made a decision that changed the trajectory of physical therapy in Bend, Oregon. After nearly a decade of practicing as a physical therapist and seeing firsthand how high patient volumes and aide-dependent models compromised care, she founded Resolve Physical Therapy with a radical commitment: every patient would receive true one-on-one care, every visit, start to finish.

Jenny earned her Doctorate of Physical Therapy from Idaho State University in 2010. She has spent her career refining a hands-on, evidence-based approach that combines manual therapy, patient education, and advanced technology. As one of the few clinics in Central Oregon utilizing DorsaVi wearable sensors and AxIT force plates for biomechanical analysis, Resolve PT brings data-driven precision to every treatment plan.

What started as a one-therapist practice has grown into a team of four specialist DPTs who share Jenny's philosophy: treat the root cause, educate the patient, and build a plan for lasting results. Every therapist at Resolve lives the active Central Oregon lifestyle, from trail running and mountain biking to skiing and paddle boarding. They don't just understand rehabilitation in theory — they understand what it means to get back to the trail, the river, and the mountain.

Jenny McAteer, DPT — Founder and Owner

What We Stand For

True 1:1 Care

You will never be handed off to an aide or assistant. From your first evaluation to your final session, you work directly with your Doctor of Physical Therapy. Your therapist's full attention, every minute, every visit.

Evidence-Based Practice

Every treatment decision is grounded in current research and clinical evidence. We use DorsaVi wearable sensors, video gait analysis, and validated outcome measures to ensure your care is data-driven and effective.

Patient Education

We believe informed patients achieve better outcomes. Your DPT will teach you the mechanics behind your condition, give you the tools to manage your body, and empower you with a personalized home program for lasting results.

Our Clinic

Located at 1740 NW Pence Ln, Suite 5 in Bend, Oregon, our clinic is equipped with the tools and space to provide comprehensive physical therapy care — including a dedicated running assessment area with DorsaVi sensor technology and AxIT force plates.
