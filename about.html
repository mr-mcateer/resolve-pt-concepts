Our Story

Personalized Care, Rooted in Bend

Resolve Physical Therapy was founded by Jenny McAteer with a clear mission: to create a culture of one-on-one personalized care where every patient receives the full attention they deserve. Located on the westside of Bend, Oregon, our clinic is known for its welcoming environment that makes physical therapy something you actually look forward to.

We believe in finding the source of your problem, not just treating symptoms. Our approach combines patient education, proper exercise prescription, and hands-on manual therapy to get you lasting results. Whether you are recovering from an orthopedic injury, motor vehicle accident, work injury, or post-surgical procedure, we have the expertise to guide your recovery.

Our team specializes in Running Rehab, Chronic Pain management, and Joint Replacement recovery, helping Bend's active community stay moving and live strong.
