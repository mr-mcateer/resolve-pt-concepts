What We Offer

Comprehensive Physical Therapy Services

Personalized treatment plans designed to get you back to your active lifestyle in Bend, Oregon.

Post-Operative Care

Physical therapy following surgeries on hip, knee, shoulder, wrist, neck, foot, ankle, and spine. Personalized recovery programs to help you get back to your active lifestyle faster. We work closely with your surgeon's protocol while pushing you to reach your full potential.

  • Hip Surgery
  • Knee Surgery
  • Shoulder Surgery
  • Spine Surgery
  • ACL Reconstruction
  • Rotator Cuff Repair
  • Joint Replacement

Injury Rehabilitation

Physical therapy intervention for a wide range of injuries and conditions. From lower back pain to sports injuries to motor vehicle accident recovery. We find the source of your pain and create a targeted treatment plan.

  • Back Pain
  • Sports Injuries
  • Motor Vehicle Accidents
  • Work Injuries
  • Neck Pain
  • Running Injuries

Manual Therapy

Hands-on treatment of underperforming muscles, tendons, ligaments, and joints. We improve mobility and function of tissues, nerves, and muscles through skilled manual techniques.

  • Soft Tissue Mobilization
  • Joint Mobilization
  • Myofascial Release
  • Trigger Point Therapy

Vestibular Rehab Therapy

Exercise-based program designed by specialty-trained vestibular physical therapists to improve balance and reduce problems related to dizziness. Customized to your specific vestibular condition.

  • Vertigo
  • Dizziness
  • Balance Issues
  • BPPV

Shockwave Therapy

Now offering focused shockwave therapy - a non-invasive treatment that uses acoustic waves to stimulate healing. Effective for chronic tendon issues, plantar fasciitis, and other stubborn conditions that haven't responded to traditional treatment.

  • Plantar Fasciitis
  • Tendinopathy
  • Chronic Pain
  • Tennis Elbow
